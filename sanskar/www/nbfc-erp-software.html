Loan Origination System (LOS) Features

The LOS module helps NBFCs manage the pre-sanction phase of a loan lifecycle, ensuring speed, compliance, and decision-making accuracy.

Multi-Channel Lead Capture

Intake from website, mobile app, agents, branches, third-party integrations (e.g., Lead Square)

Auto-lead assignment based on geography, product, or agent

Customer Onboarding & KYC Management

Capture of applicant and co-applicant details

Upload and verification of PAN, Aadhaar, Voter ID, Passport, etc.

e-KYC and video KYC integrations

Real-time validation using APIs (e.g., NSDL, UIDAI)

Credit Bureau Integration

Fetching credit scores and reports from bureaus like CIBIL, Experian

Rule-based credit scoring engine

Flagging of risk indicators (defaults, high DTI, poor history)

Document Management

Configurable document checklist by loan type

Digital document upload (PDF, image)

OCR and auto-extraction of document data

Version control & secure access

Eligibility & Risk Assessment Engine

Auto-calculation of eligibility based on income, obligations, age, credit score

Custom scoring models and weightage configurations

Red flag alerts for fraudulent or high-risk applications

Field Investigation Workflow

Assign FI task to field agent

Capture PD (Personal Discussion) questionnaire

Geotagging and photo capture

Real-time reporting to credit manager

TNC Compliance & Internal Checklists

Rule-based "Yes/No" compliance matrix

Mandate checks (CIBIL score threshold, KYC verified, FI done, etc.)

Auto-hold or rejection for non-compliant leads

Credit Appraisal Memo (CAM) Generation

Automated CAM preparation from lead data

Risk summary, income analysis, loan proposal, and sanction terms

Approval workflow with digital signatures

Sanction & Disbursement Preparation

Multiple approval levels (underwriting, credit head, legal)

Auto-sanction letter generation with template

Bank account verification and fund readiness checks

Loan statement generation

Disbursement Management
